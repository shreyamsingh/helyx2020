About this project

Arguably the most dangerous impact we have on dry land as a species is our discarded plastic or otherwise non-biodegradable refuse that often ends up in our natural habitats, mostly near urban centers or highways. These wastes often contain harmful chemicals that can seep into the soil and in turn into the groundwater, harming all organisms that rely on that water, whether directly or indirectly. This project's goal is to provide an incentive for people who come across this waste to either mark it for pickup, earning whoever rises to the challenge points, or to pick it up themselves, earning points in the process. A leaderboard is provided to offer further incentive and friendly competition in the spirit of cleanup throughout the world.
